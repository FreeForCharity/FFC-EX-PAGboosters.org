Our Mission

PAG Parents is dedicated to promoting and advancing amateur athletics, with a special focus on the Phoenix Academy of Performing Arts competitive gymnastics team.

 

Our mission is to ensure the welfare and success of our gymnasts through various initiatives, including fundraising, corporate sponsorships, and community support.

 

We work closely with PAPAPA to provide the resources and opportunities needed for our athletes to thrive.

 

 

Our Initiatives

Key Programs

Fundraising Events

Corporate Sponsorships

Training Camps

We sponsor specialized training camps to help our gymnasts hone their skills and prepare for competitions.

Scholarship Programs

Our scholarship programs support talented gymnasts in pursuing their athletic and academic goals.
