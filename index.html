About PAG Booster

Dedicated to supporting the Phoenix Academy of Performing Arts competitive gymnastics team

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure the welfare and success of our athletes by providing financial support, organizing fundraising events, and fostering a strong community of parents and supporters.

What We Do

We support the Phoenix Academy of Performing Arts gymnastics team through various fundraising programs, promoting the benefits of gymnastics and athletic development in our community.

Our dedicated team of parents and volunteers works tirelessly to create opportunities for our young athletes to compete at the highest levels while developing character, discipline, and lifelong skills.

Our Programs

Supporting our gymnasts through targeted program funding

Program 1: Phoenix Academy USAG Xcel Team Regular Season (Core Program)

PRIMARY FOCUS

Focused on providing direct support to the active pipeline of girls from pre-team through Sapphire. Helps ensure there are sufficient gymnasts in the program to field a team. This includes paying meet fees and support for all regular season USAG Xcel meets including annual and monthly team fees.

Support Core Program

Program 2: Phoenix Academy USAG Xcel Team Fly-away Optional Team Meet

Help send a portion of our team to an out-of-state meet in Tampa, Florida. These larger meets in major cities outside of Region 7 help expand State College gymnastics beyond regional boundaries and provide valuable competitive experience.

Program 3: Phoenix Academy USAG Xcel PA State Competition

Focused on funding the meet fees and gymnast support for those gymnasts that qualified for the Pennsylvania USAG state competition. Help our talented gymnasts compete at the state level and make their dreams possible.

Send PAG to States

Program 4: Phoenix Academy USAG Xcel PA Region 7 Regional Competition

Help fund our most elite gymnasts who have qualified for the rigorous Regional meet. This is the culminating meet of the postseason and represents the highest level of achievement in our region.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PAG Booster

You are a member when your gymnast is accepted to the team. Reach out to the booster club to get involved and stay connected with our community.

Sponsoring PAG Booster offers numerous benefits, including brand exposure at our events, recognition on our website, and the satisfaction of supporting young athletes in their gymnastics journey.

You can make a donation through our website by contacting us directly using the form above or calling 814-470-5911. All contributions are greatly appreciated and go towards supporting our gymnasts and their activities.
